The Basics

Minesweeper is a logic puzzle where you uncover squares in a grid to find hidden mines, using numbers on revealed squares to deduce mine locations; left-click to reveal, right-click (or long-press on mobile) to flag suspected mines, and use the numbers (indicating adjacent mines) to clear safe squares and avoid losing. The first click is always safe and often clears a large area, while numbers show mines in the 8 surrounding squares, helping you logically flag mines and uncover more squares until the board is clear or a mine is hit.
